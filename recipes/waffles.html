WAFFLES RECIPE

Description

A great breakfast and brunch recipe to get your day started

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups milk, or more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • Â½ cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. step 1

    Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.

  2. step 2

    Whisk eggs together in a large bowl until fluffy. Mix in 2 cups milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract until combined.

  3. step 3

    Combine 2 cups flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Slowly pour flour mixture into egg mixture, stirring as you pour. Mix until few to no lumps remain, being careful not to over mix. Add more flour if batter is too thin, or more milk if batter is too sticky or thick.

  4. step 4

    Ladle some of the batter onto the preheated waffle iron. Cook until waffle is golden brown and the iron stops steaming, 2 to 5 minutes.
